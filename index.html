Theobroma means 'Food of the Gods' in Greek, befitting our exclusive and indulgent range of offerings that include brownies, cakes, desserts, chocolates, breads and savouries. From our humble beginnings in 2004, when the first Theobroma pastry store opened its doors at the iconic Cusrow Baug at Colaba Causeway (Mumbai), we've grown to become a Pan-India chain of patisseries with stores in over 18 cities.
Our mission is to spread happiness by serving smiles on a plate, and this journey continues as we open Theobroma patisseries across the country.As we all know, sweets are replaced with cakes and for every occasion, we look for some decadent cakes that can make the day worth cherishing. Bakingo has made this possible.
Now, you just have to name the occasion and we will serve a cake before you. We understand the depth of every occasion that you are about to celebrate and therefore, we try our best to make your feast remarkable with our incredible cakes. Also, you can get the cake delivery in Surat within 2 hours that makes our delivery service also worth appreciating.
